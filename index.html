About me

A bite-size description.

  • I am leading the ongoing development of the GENOSCORES project - a web platform and software tools for the calculation of genotypic predictors (scores) of binary and quantitative phenotypes. I am working on deploying GENOSCORES as a publicly available research tool.
  • I hold PhD in Computational/Applied mechanics. My PhD research was focused on a multidisciplinary area on the intersection between computer science, data modelling and analysis, computational/applied mechanics and biology of the cell.
